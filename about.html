About Us

Give and Take is a non-profit organization that aims to redistribute basic utilities like food, books, clothes, medicines, etc. We believe in "One man's trash, another man's treasure" and thrive forward to facilitate its enforcement. We have partnered with leading NGOs in more than 20 cities across India. This is where the people can donate anything like clothes, books or medicines and we ensure that it will help the needy ones.
